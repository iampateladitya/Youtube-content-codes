Gadar 2: The Katha Continues

·  2023  ·  2h 45m

Gadar 2 was theatrically released on 11 August 2023, where it grossed over ₹659.68 crore (US$83 million) worldwide and emerged as the year's second highest-grossing Indian film of 2023 and second highest-grossing Hindi film of 2023. It also became the sixth highest-grossing Hindi film of all-time.
